Meet The Team

Behind the Brand

It started with a love for cars. Reed has always had a thing for them — the lines, the craft, the way a clean car just hits different. Watching well-kept vehicles roll by always lit something up, and the idea of bringing that same level of care to other people's rides turned from a passing thought into a real plan.

In 2026, Reed teamed up with Tysyn and turned that passion into Brumbaugh Detailing — a mobile auto care service built on attention to detail, quality products, and treating every car like it's our own. Whether it's a daily driver covered in mud or a weekend showpiece, we bring the same focus to every job.
